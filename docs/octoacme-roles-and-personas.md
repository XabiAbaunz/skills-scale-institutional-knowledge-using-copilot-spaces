# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

## UX Designer

> Added in response to [issue #4](https://github.com/XabiAbaunz/skills-scale-institutional-knowledge-using-copilot-spaces/issues/4)

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure the usability and user-centricity of features delivered by the team. They conduct user research, collaborate on prototypes, and vet user flows before development begins.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user interviews, usability testing, and collect feedback
- Develop wireframes and prototypes in line with project goals
- Collaborate with Developers and Product Managers to refine requirements
- Advocate for accessibility and design standards

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences
- Reduce rework by validating designs before development
- Champion usability standards across all product surfaces

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and prototype walkthroughs with Product Managers and Developers
- Usability test summaries shared with the full team
- Accessibility and design-system guidance in PR and design comments

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Works closely with **Product Managers** to refine feature requirements and align on user needs
- Partners with **Developers** to review design feasibility and implementation details
- Supports **QA** in defining usability acceptance criteria and edge cases

## Scrum Master

> Added in response to [issue #4](https://github.com/XabiAbaunz/skills-scale-institutional-knowledge-using-copilot-spaces/issues/4)

### Role Summary
The Scrum Master facilitates agile ceremonies, removes blockers, and fosters continuous improvement in delivery practices.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate standups, sprint planning, sprint reviews, and retrospectives
- Shield the team from external interruptions and unplanned work
- Coach the team on scrum practices and an agile mindset
- Track and escalate blockers as needed

### Goals
- Enable a self-organizing, high-performing team
- Reduce friction and blockers that slow delivery
- Drive continuous improvement through retrospective action items

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremony facilitation
- Blocker escalation notes to Project Manager and stakeholders
- Retrospective summaries with action item tracking

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partners with **Project Managers** on delivery planning and milestone tracking
- Supports **Developers** by clearing impediments and protecting sprint commitments
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** to ensure backlog clarity and sprint readiness

---

## Data Analyst

> Added in response to [issue #4](https://github.com/XabiAbaunz/skills-scale-institutional-knowledge-using-copilot-spaces/issues/4)

### Role Summary
Data Analysts help measure project impact, define metrics, and inform data-driven decisions throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Design dashboards and metrics tracking for features and releases
- Analyze product, process, and quality data
- Provide insights during planning and retrospectives
- Define instrumentation requirements for new features

### Goals
- Enable evidence-based decision-making across the team
- Make success metrics visible and actionable
- Surface early signals of issues or opportunities

### Typical Communication
- Dashboard and report walkthroughs during planning and retrospectives
- Ad-hoc data analyses shared via project channels or documents
- Instrumentation requirements communicated to Developers in backlog items

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Supports **Product Managers** in defining measurable outcomes and success metrics
- Works with **Developers** to instrument features for analytics
- Helps **Project Managers** monitor delivery metrics and track progress against goals

---

## Business Analyst

> Added in response to [issue #4](https://github.com/XabiAbaunz/skills-scale-institutional-knowledge-using-copilot-spaces/issues/4)

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between stakeholder needs and technical solutions, ensuring requirements are well-specified and prioritized.

### Responsibilities
- Gather, analyze, and document business requirements
- Support backlog refinement and priority alignment
- Facilitate requirements workshops and discovery sessions
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business needs

### Goals
- Ensure stakeholder needs are clearly understood and translated into actionable requirements
- Reduce ambiguity and rework caused by incomplete specifications
- Serve as a reliable bridge between business stakeholders and the delivery team

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documents and user story write-ups shared with Product Managers and Developers
- Workshop summaries and decision logs distributed to stakeholders
- Sign-off and acceptance communication during UAT and review stages

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** to clarify needs and refine acceptance criteria
- Assists **Developers** and **QA** with requirement details and edge cases
- Engages **Stakeholders** throughout planning and review phases to ensure alignment
